### Hey, I'm Lidor 👋

My job is to make sure the software nobody looks at keeps running.
Backend systems — the kind where success means nothing goes wrong
and nobody calls you, and failure means everything goes wrong
and everybody calls you.

On the side I'm studying **IT & Information Systems at USN** in Norway.
Also slowly finding my footing in **AI and ML**, which mostly means
I'm discovering new ways to be wrong about things faster than before.
